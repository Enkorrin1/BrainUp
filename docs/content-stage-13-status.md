# Content Stage 13 Status

## Goal

Define the BrainUp puzzle content system: the quality standard for future rich,
visual, individual puzzle scenes.

## Delivered

- Added `docs/puzzle-content-system.md`.
- Defined production-ready puzzle anatomy.
- Defined quality rules by age band, difficulty, and skill.
- Defined visual scene, character feedback, hint, explanation, interaction,
  animation, asset, placement, and QA rules.
- Added readiness levels from draft to production.

## Why It Matters

BrainUp now has a shared standard for creating many puzzles without making them
feel repetitive or low quality. Future content packs can be checked against
this document before they enter the learning route.

## Next Step

Stage 14 should create `docs/puzzle-type-matrix.md`, using this system to define
25 concrete puzzle families and their visual/interactive requirements.
